The Roadmap

What we're actively building towards to make GreenBrowser even better.

Local AI Integration

We plan to add full local AI integration in the near future. (Note: Currently in early testing, many bugs exist)

Carbon/Pollution Calculator

A built-in tool that calculates how much your session and/or search is polluting the planet.

Tracker & Cookie Blocker

We plan to add a native tracker-blocker (or cookie-blocker) to guarantee absolute privacy.

Account Module

Currently in conception. An account system so that you can carry your bookmarks securely everywhere you go.

News & Fun Facts

Lot more functionalities planned, including curated news threads and interesting fun facts about Earth.

Emergency Alerting System

Provide a location manually to be alerted of future wildfires and fire risks in your zone with our EAS.
